Sunny and April

Inspired by our own love for outdoor exploration, we embarked on a journey to create a one-of-a-kind water rafting company that would bring the joy and excitement of rafting to enthusiasts and thrill-seekers alike. With our extensive knowledge of the region's majestic rivers and untamed landscapes, we've crafted unforgettable experiences that will leave you breathless and craving for more.

History

Koki and Aubrey a dynamic and ambitious couple, shared a vision of creating a rafting company that would make a positive experience on people's lives.

Today, Sunny and April Rafting stands as a testament to their devotion of making a postiive experience to people's lives. spirit of Koki and Aubrey. Their vision and determination have not only enriched the lives of countless adventurers but also contributed to the local economy and the conservation of our natural wonders.
